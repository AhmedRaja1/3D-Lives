The Rise of 3D Lives

3D Lives is a collection of NFTs that is launched with the ambition of empowering lives using technology. Healthcare has always been the noisiest concern of mankind as they say "If you have life, you have the world". Healthcare is a fundamental right and should be made available to everyone all around the globe.

The time has come that bleeding edge technologies like blockchain and non-fungible tokens should play their parts in uplifting the standards of the healthcare sector.

OpenSea Discord

High Quality NFT Collection

With this vision in our brains, we have launched a collection of NFTs with 3D faces of humans, which is a dataset used in one of our top-priority healthcare projects. Empowering healthcare means empowering the life on the planet & making it a great place to live. We want these NFTs as tokens to provide leverage to the healthcare industry, the revenue will be used for funding healthcare organizations, biotech startups and providing free technology resources for healthcare projects & the opportunities are endless.
